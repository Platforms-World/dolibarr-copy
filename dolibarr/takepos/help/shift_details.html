  • راجع أي فرق قبل تأكيد الإغلاق النهائي.

English Guide

What is this screen for?

This page shows shift details such as opening cash, sales, withdrawals, and the expected closing total.

How do you use it?

  • Review total transactions recorded in the shift.
  • Check cash and non-cash payments.
  • Review any Paid In, Paid Out, or Safe Drop movements.
  • Compare expected total with actual cash.
  • Use this page before closing or for later review.

Helpful tips

  • Shift details are important during staff handover.
  • Review any difference before final close confirmation.
